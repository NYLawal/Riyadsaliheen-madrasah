Who We Are

As a people, we prioritise continuous learning, fostering a culture of mutual support in making Arabic and Islamic education available and affordable to everyone, both young and old.

:عرضنا

تعليم اللغة العربية وما ينتمى إليها والدراسات الإسلامية الصحيحة وتحفيظ القرأن الكريم مع التجويد

Our Offer

Sound Arabic and Islamic education covering all aspects of religious upliftment, including Arabic speaking, reading and writing skills, Arabic language vocabulary development, Qur'an recitation and memorisation with proper tajweed, character building and public speaking practicality.

:فريقنا

يهتم فريقنا اهتماما بليغا بأعمال إدارية محكمة مع المدرسين الأكفاء ، نقوم بتربية الأطفال تربية إسلامية ثقافية سليمة التي تساعد لتوطيد العلاقة بينهم وبين ربهم و الآخرين

Our Team

With a team of concerned administrators, efficient teachers, and caring staff, we embark on a journey to nurture young minds from their early age with sound Islamic and Arabic education that help them develop and maintain a healthy relationship with their Lord and fellow humans.
